# 📊 Retail Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze retail sales data and provide actionable business insights. The dashboard focuses on key performance indicators, regional performance, and profitability analysis to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objective
- Analyze overall sales and profitability  
- Track key business KPIs  
- Identify regional and product-level performance  
- Provide a clear and interactive view of business data  

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Power BI  
- Data Visualization  
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)  
- Data Modeling  

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features
- **KPI Cards**: Total Sales, Total Profit, Profit Margin  
- **Sales Analysis Table**: Brand-wise performance with conditional formatting  
- **Geographical Insights**: Sales distribution across US states (map visualization)  
- **Key Influencers Analysis**: Identifies factors impacting operating profit  

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Sales and profit show strong correlation across regions  
- Certain brands contribute significantly to overall revenue  
- Profitability varies across different product segments  
- Geographic trends highlight high-performing regions  

---

## 💡 Business Impact
- Helps in understanding revenue and profit drivers  
- Supports strategic decision-making using visual insights  
- Identifies areas for performance improvement  
- Enables better planning and resource allocation  

---
